About Me

Engineering excellence through precision, innovation, and unwavering commitment to structural integrity.

I am a highly experienced civil engineer with a deep specialization in structural steel design and execution. With a B.Sc. in Civil Engineering from BUET (Class of 2015, Batch of 2009), I have contributed to over 150 industrial and power plant projects, both in Bangladesh and internationally.

My expertise lies in applying American Building Codes (AISC, ASCE, AWS, UBC, ACI) and international structural standards, ensuring the highest levels of safety, efficiency, and cost-effectiveness in every design. I am passionate about delivering engineering solutions that stand the test of time.

I specialize in structural retrofitting and rehabilitation, multidisciplinary design coordination, and solving complex design challenges with practical, real-world engineering solutions that prioritize both structural integrity and economic viability.
